Within the active `value-boardroom` session, switch to the **`cfo`** role.

Consult `references/operating-roles.md` for the `cfo` remit (profit quality, leverage, working capital, capital allocation), required operating evidence, FCF transmission path, and incentive conflict. Then take **one role turn** following the boardroom turn contract: state a fact or labelled assumption, surface one incentive conflict or resource demand, trace a clear cash-flow path, and ask exactly one question. Never invent internal metrics.
